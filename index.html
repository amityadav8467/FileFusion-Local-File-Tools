About FileFusion

Your Privacy-First File Toolkit

We believe that fundamental file tasks shouldn't require you to upload your personal data to unknown servers. FileFusion was built from the ground up to respect your privacy by leveraging the power of your own browser.

Using cutting-edge technologies like WebAssembly (WASM), all processing is done locally on your machine. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just someone who needs to get things done quickly, FileFusion is your secure and reliable partner for everyday file management.

Core Principles

  • 100% Client-Side

    Your files are never uploaded. Total privacy guaranteed.

  • Blazing Fast

    Operations run at the speed of your local machine, with no network latency.

  • No Installation Needed

    Works directly in any modern browser. Just open the file and go.

  • Offline Capable

    Use all tools anytime, anywhere, even without an internet connection.
